About The Conference

AiConference.io brings together the brightest minds in artificial intelligence for three days of learning, networking, and innovation. Our 2023 event will feature over 100 speakers, 50+ sessions, and numerous networking opportunities with industry leaders.

This year's theme is "Responsible AI for Global Impact," focusing on how AI can be developed and deployed ethically to solve real-world problems across industries.

Key conference information:

  • Dates: October 15-17, 2023
  • Location: Moscone Center, San Francisco
  • Expected attendees: 5,000+
  • Format: Keynotes, breakout sessions, workshops, and networking events
